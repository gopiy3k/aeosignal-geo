Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2025

At Aeosignal.Space, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1️⃣ Information We Collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, website URL (when you submit forms).
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, IP address.
  • Cookies: For functionality and analytics.

2️⃣ How We Use Your Information:

  • To respond to your inquiries.
  • To provide and improve our services.
  • To send occasional updates (you can opt-out anytime).

3️⃣ Data Sharing:

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with trusted service providers for analytics and service delivery under strict confidentiality.

4️⃣ Data Security:

We use secure protocols to protect your data.

5️⃣ Your Rights:

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us at contact@aeosignal.space.

6️⃣ Changes:

We may update this policy and will notify you on this page.

Contact:

If you have questions, email us at contact@aeosignal.space.
