About Gopinath

Started in tech support, took a left turn into 3AM crypto futures, and somehow ended up building NFTs, ERC tokens, ICO sites, and even a trading bot that occasionally reminds him who’s boss. He’s not just a builder; he’s the outreach guy, the growth hacker, and the lead-gen engine (translation: sends polite emails until you reply). He wears every hat: founder, coder, marketer, support, sometimes all at once. Still building, still learning, like the AIs he wrangles… only he runs on coffee, not GPUs.

Today, he's the founder of AEO Signal, building tools to solve a problem he saw firsthand: in a world of AI chatbots, brands need a new way to be discovered.
